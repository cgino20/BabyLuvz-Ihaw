About

BabyLuvz Ihaw is a street food service that serves a healthy, clean, and delicious street foods along Gumamela Street. BabyLuvz Ihaw promised to deliver a better cutomer service and the cleanliness of the foods we serve and the surrounding of the store.

Our Foods

We serve a variety of tasty street foods yet healthy ones like Chicken Intestine, Kwek-kwek, Fishballs, kikiam, Chicken Inasal, Pork Barbeque, Hotdog, Squidballs, Calamares, Beverages, and many more. We also serve meals and miryenda for everyone.
